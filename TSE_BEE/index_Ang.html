Who are we?

Bienvenue sur le site de l'Economie expérimentale et comportementale-TSE

Experimental economics is a scientific method for reconstructing decision-making contexts in the laboratory. This group brings together researchers and TSE students working on behavioral economics, experimental economics, as well as the interdisciplinary themes of psychology, sociology, neuroscience, biology and political science.
