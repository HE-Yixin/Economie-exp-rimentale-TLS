EXPERIENCE EXPERIMENTAL ECONOMY in June at the Toulouse School of Economics Researchers from the Toulouse School of Economics are looking for participants for two new paid studies in June. Both experiments will take place at the Manufacture des Tabacs

FIRST EXPERIENCE :

For this first experience, each participant will be paid on average 12 €.
The experience lasts less than 1 hour.
Interested persons may only register in one session.

If you wish to participate, please tell us your availability (day and time), your name, your first name by contacting me at the following address:

experience.economie.experimentale@tse-fr.eu

We will confirm your participation by e-mail.

TUESDAY JUNE 04 : 9h30 à 10h30 10h30 à 11h30 11h30 à 12h30 14h à 15h 15h à 16h 16h à 17h



SECOND EXPERIENCE :

For this second experience, each participant will be remunerated on average between € 15 and € 20 (minimum € 5) depending on their decisions and those of other participants.
The experience lasts approximately 1h30

Interested persons may only register in one session.Participants must be fluent in the English language, even if all oral explanations are given in French.

If you wish to participate, please tell us your availability (day and time), your name, your first name by contacting me at the following address:

experience.economie.experimentale@tse-fr.eu

We will confirm your participation by e-mail.
