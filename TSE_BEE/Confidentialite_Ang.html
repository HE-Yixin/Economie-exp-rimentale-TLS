Confidentiality:

About your information

When you register, you must give us only personal information. We only use this data to organize our experiences, they will never be passed on to third parties. You can unsubscribe from our database at any time by contacting us or by using the link proposed in your registration emails and in this case you will not be invited.

However, if you change your mind in the future, we ask you to re-register with the same email address to retrieve your history.

The data is used for our scientific work and our publications. Your decisions will remain anonymous: we are not interested in the identity of the participants.

We keep track of the experiences you have participated in so you do not invite you to a similar experience. We also record the number of times you registered but did not come.
